Bangalore is a beautiful and historic city in the state of Karnataka in India. Now called Bengaluru, the city changed its' name in 2014 officially as a symbol of independence from the British who had renamed many cities in India to be more anglicized. Bangalore is India's third most populated city and is known as India's tech hub and stands out as one of the top ten entrepreneurial locations in the world.
